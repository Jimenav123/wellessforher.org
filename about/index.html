ABOUT

Hi, I’m Jimena.

Wellness For Her started because I was tired of feeling like nothing was actually working.

For years, I dealt with PCOS, hormonal imbalances, acne, bloating — all of it. And every solution felt temporary or completely disconnected from what my body actually needed.

At some point, I stopped looking for quick fixes and started paying attention.

What started as frustration turned into a deeper understanding of how to support my body in a way that felt realistic. Not extreme. Not overwhelming. Just consistent and intentional.

Now, I’m combining that experience with a background in Health Science and Business, along with my MindBodyGreen Health Coach Certification.

This space is where I share what actually works — the things I wish I had known earlier.

If you’re trying to feel better in your body without overcomplicating everything, you’re in the right place.
