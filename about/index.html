Hi, I’m Jimena :)

I started Wellness For Her because I was honestly just tired of feeling like nothing was working.

I’ve dealt with PCOS, hormonal imbalances, acne, bloating… all of it. And every time I went to a doctor, I felt like I was getting the same answers.

Try this. Take this. Go on birth control.

And it just never felt like it was actually fixing anything.

So I started figuring things out on my own.

What started as frustration turned into me really learning how to support my body in a way that actually made sense. Not extreme. Not overwhelming. Just small things that actually helped.

Now I’m combining that with what I’ve studied. I have a degree in Health Science and Business, and I’m currently getting my health coaching certification through MindBodyGreen.

This space is basically everything I wish I had earlier.

If you’ve ever felt like your body is confusing or like nothing is working… I get it.
